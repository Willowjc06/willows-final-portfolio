In my other modules, I am designing a 2D animated video on climate change, addressing the increasing risks of single-use plastic. In Creative Coding, I am composing 3 coded designs of my using 3 differing principals.

It was my first submission for the module, and I am very proud of the piece due to the fact that I had never explore the 3D modelling world, so doing this was a big challenge, but it turned out to be fun, and taught me a lot about 3D compositions, and the use of geometric shapes.
